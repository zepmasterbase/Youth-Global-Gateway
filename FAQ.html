Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your journey.

Is Youth Global Gateway a legitimate agency?

Yes. We are committed to transparent processes and connecting applicants with verified opportunities abroad.

Do you guarantee a job?

We provide job placement support and connect you with employers, but final hiring decisions are made by employers and visa authorities.

How long does the process take?

Processing times vary depending on the country and visa requirements, typically ranging from a few weeks to several months.

What documents do I need?

Usually a valid passport, CV, educational certificates, and other documents depending on the destination country.

Can I pay in installments?

Payment options may be available depending on the package. Contact us to discuss flexible arrangements.

Which countries can I apply to?

We currently support opportunities in Portugal, Canada, Ireland, Germany, Latvia, Czech Republic, and Ecuador.

Do you assist with visas?

Yes, we provide guidance and support throughout the visa application process.

Will you help after I arrive?

Premium package clients receive post-arrival support to help with settling in and adjusting.
